    Ram Jethmalani
    Indian lawyer
    Ram Boolchand Jethmalani is an Indian lawyer and politician. He has served as India's Union Law Minister and as chairman of the Bar Council of India. Wikipedia
    Born: 14 September 1923 (age 93), Shikarpur, Sindh, Pakistan
    Spouse: Ratna R. (m. 1947), Durga Jethmalani
    Education: S.C. Shahani Law College, Government Law College, Mumbai
    Books: Maverick Unchanged, Unrepentant, Conscience of a Maverick
    Political parties: Bharatiya Janata Party (?2013), Rashtriya Janata Dal (2016)
    Children: Mahesh Jethmalani, Shobha Jethmalani, Rani Jethmalani, Janak Jethmalani
